The parents who will do anything to get their children into the right School

Story Line

A couple from Chandni Chowk aspire to give their daughter the best education and thus be a part of and accepted by the elite of Delhi. In a society obsessed with education, Raj and Meeta strive to secure a seat for their daughter in a prestigious school. However, their journey turns into a hilarious roller coaster ride as they try to navigate the complexities of the system.
